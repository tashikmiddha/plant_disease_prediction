Privacy Policy - PlantDoc

Your trust is our priority

Introduction

At PlantDoc, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered plant disease prediction services.

What We Collect

When you use PlantDoc, we may collect:

  • Plant images uploaded by users
  • Basic device/browser data for performance optimization
  • Optional contact info (if you register or contact us)

How We Use Your Data

We use uploaded plant images solely to run disease detection models. We do not store images unless explicitly agreed by you for research or improvement purposes.

We may analyze usage patterns (without identifying individuals) to improve model accuracy, user experience, and system performance.

If you provide an email address, we may send you updates about the app or respond to inquiries.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • You can request deletion of your uploaded images.
  • You may opt-out of data sharing for research.
  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure protocols to protect your information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Changes to Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of any major changes and always maintain transparency.
